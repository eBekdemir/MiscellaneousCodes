Mustafa Kemal Ataturk

Founding Father of the Republic of Turkey

1st President of Turkey

Ataturk at Kocatepe

Here's a time line of Ataturk's life:

  • 1881, Ataturk born in Salonica (Selanik), Ottoman Empire.
  • 1893, Mustafa enters military preparatory school in Salonica.
  • 1899, Mustafa Kemal enters infantry class of War College in Istanbul.
  • 1902, Commissioned Second Lieutenant, and enters Staff College.
  • 1905, Passes out as Staff Captain and is posted for Fifth Army in Syria; revives a secret opposition group in Damascus.
  • 1907, Promoted Adjutant-Major, Posted to Third Army headquarters in Salonica.
  • 1908, Mustafa Kemal travels to Tripoli and Benghazi to re-establish CUP.
  • 1909, Travels with his division to the outskirts of Constantinople; 31 March Incident.
  • 1910, Visits French army manoeuvres in Picardy.
  • 1911, Volunteers for service against the Italians in Cyrenaica.
  • 1911, Promoted Major.
  • 1912, Salonica falls to the Greeks, Mustafa Kemal leaves Cyrenaica and returns to Istanbul.
  • 1913, Mustafa Kemal appointed military attaché in Sofia.
  • 1914, Mustafa Kemal promoted Lieutenant-Colonel.
  • 1915, Mustafa Kemal leaves Sofia to take up appointment as commander of 19th division for service in Battle of Gallipoli.
  • 1915, Allied troops Landing at Anzac Cove faced with Kemal.
  • 1915, He become the Hero of Suvla Bay.
  • 1919, Appointed the Inspector of the Ninth Army Troops.
  • 1919, 19 May, Kemal lands in Samsun.
  • 1919, 8 July, Mustafa Kemal resigns from the post of Inspector of Third Army and from the army.
  • 1919, Kemal elected Chairman of Erzurum Congress.
  • 1920, Kemal opens the Grand National Assembly (BMM) in Ankara.
  • 1920, Kemal is condemned to death by the government in Constantinople.
  • 1921, Appointed Commander - in - Chief by the Grand National Assembly.
  • 1921, 23 August, The Battle of Sakarya begins with Turkish troops led by Mustafa Kemal.
  • 1921, 18 September, Turks won The Battle of Sakarya and Kemal receives the rank of Marshal and the title Gazi (veteran, victorious warrior).
  • 1922, Gazi Mustafa Kemal led Great Offensive, and Turks won The Battle of Dumlupinar.
  • 1922, 10 September, Turks enters Izmir under his leadership.
  • 1922, 1 November, The Grand National Assembly abolish the Sultanate.
  • 1923, 29 October, Proclamation of the Republic of Turkey, and then he was elected first President.
  • 1938, 10 November, He died leaving behind a great legacy.
